Mama's (aka me) Marinade

Description

This recipe is great for your favortite cut of steak, chicken or pork! It has even been tried and true on veggies.

A combination of savory, a hint smokiness that leaves your mouth watering for more.

Description
  • Worchestire Sauce
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Garlic Powder
  • Lemon Pepper
  • Season Salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Brown Sugar
  • Maple Syrup

Optional: Orange Sauce

Steps
  1. Get your ingriedients together
  2. Mix all of the ingriedients. I prefer to mix them directly the baggy I will be marinading in.
  3. That's it, you're done!
